SOLARIS

Solaris is a Twin-stick shooter where the player moves through an abandoned laboratory and shoots robots and collect keycards to progress. You will find some keycards hidden in a room and some dropped from robots. In this game I have also made a separate endless game mode where 50 waves of robots spawn and you have to survive kind of like COD zombies, I implemented a money system which you can unlock doors with and unlock more spawn points for the robots. the robots cant spawn in the same room which the player is in.

Shmup

Shmup is a Shoot em up where there is a large amount of enemies coming at the player, with lots of projectiles going through the air and the player needs to dodge these projectiles while eliminating the enemies. In this game the player is a submarine and kills squids and sharks with torpedos to get to the end. At the end is a boss battle against a big rock monster.

About Me

Hello! I’m Raphael Lariccia. My love for game programming started in Roblox, when I experimented and tried to make a game in Roblox Studio I suddenly wanted to create games. I have a large creative mind and I saw in game developing that I could make use of that creativity.

Programming appeals to me because I like to see how things work and create them myself, it’s very interesting to me.

I have gamed Since I was a little kid and I still do it for multiple hours every week, It’s something that I enjoy doing. It feels amazing to finally be able to make my own games and make my dreams come true.

I also really enjoy sword fighting, although I’m still a beginner I find it fascinating
